2. Refund Policy

2.1 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

New Subscribers: We offer a full refund within 30 days of your first subscription payment, no questions asked.

Eligibility requirements:

  • First-time subscriber to paid plan
  • Request made within 30 days of initial payment
  • Account in good standing (no Terms violations)

2.2 Refund Scenarios

Scenario Refund Eligibility Processing Time
First 30 days (new subscriber) 100% Full Refund 5-7 business days

Service outage (>24 hours) Pro-rated credit Automatic to account

2.3 Non-Refundable Items

The following are NOT eligible for refunds:

  • Partial month subscriptions after 30-day period
  • Annual plans after 30 days (access continues for full year)
  • One-time purchases or credits
  • Fees paid to third parties (payment processing fees)
  • Services after account termination for policy violations
  • Add-on features or extra storage purchased

5. Special Circumstances

5.1 Service Interruptions

If our service experiences extended downtime:

  • < 24 hours: Service extension equal to downtime
  • 24-72 hours: 7-day service credit
  • > 72 hours: Pro-rated refund for affected period

5.2 Billing Errors

If you notice incorrect charges:

  • Notify us within 60 days of the charge
  • We'll investigate and correct within 5 business days
  • Refund any overcharges immediately
  • Prevent future occurrences

5.3 Account Disputes

If you dispute a charge with your bank:

  • Please contact us first to resolve the issue
  • Chargebacks may result in account suspension
  • We'll work with you to find a solution
  • Most issues can be resolved without chargebacks
